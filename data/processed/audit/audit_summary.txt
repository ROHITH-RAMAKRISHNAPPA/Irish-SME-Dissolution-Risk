AUDIT CSVS: purpose
==================================================
Each audit CSV contains, per company:
  - obs_date and label used during training/testing
  - model's calibrated risk score and predicted tier
  - the company's CURRENT CRO status (real-world truth)
  - whether the modelled outcome matches today's reality

Use this when an examiner asks for evidence that:
  - the model trained on companies that actually did dissolve, OR
  - flagged companies have, in fact, dissolved since obs_date.
